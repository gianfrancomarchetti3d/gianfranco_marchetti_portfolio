Gianfranco Marchetti

Professional 3D Artist | Calcinate (BG), Italy

I specialize in architectural visualization, interior and exterior rendering, and product visualization — crafting compelling and immersive 3D experiences.

Over the years, I have collaborated with international clients and video game teams, producing detailed 3D environments, assets, and cinematic compositions optimized for games, animation, marketing, and architectural presentations.

My work focuses on photorealistic rendering, PBR materials, realistic lighting, and seamless integration of CGI with real-world photography. I aim to create visuals that are both technically precise and emotionally engaging.

I provide full consulting services for visual communication and design projects, guaranteeing high-quality results, punctuality, and meticulous attention to detail in every stage of production.
