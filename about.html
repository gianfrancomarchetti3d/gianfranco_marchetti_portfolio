Chi sono

My name is Gianfranco Marchetti, and I’m a 3D Consultant based in Calcinate (BG), Italy.
I specialize in architectural visualization, interior/exterior rendering, product rendering, VFX and 3D character/creature modeling & animation.

I’ve collaborated with international game developers and artists, creating detailed and atmospheric 3D environments that tell emotional stories.
I’m also part of the Blender Italia Association and administrator of the Blender Italia User Group Bergamo.
