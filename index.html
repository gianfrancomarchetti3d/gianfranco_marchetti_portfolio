Gianfranco Marchetti

3D Consultant | Calcinate (BG), Italy

I’m a 3D artist specialized in modeling, texturing, and rendering with Blender.

I create realistic product visualizations and detailed environments using PBR workflows and efficient lighting setups.

My goal is to deliver visually strong and technically clean 3D assets ready for games, animation, or marketing visualization.

I’m available for freelance collaborations and remote work — feel free to contact me for projects, partnerships, or commissions.

Specialized in architectural visualization, interior/exterior rendering, and product rendering — crafting compelling visuals that tell emotional and immersive stories.

Collaborated with an international video game development team, designing highly detailed and atmospheric 3D environments — refining skills in composition, lighting, texturing, and visual storytelling.

Active member of Blender Italia Association and administrator of the Blender Italia User Group of Bergamo, promoting the professional culture of 3D art and visualization.

Excited to contribute to projects in game-art production, environment creation, and product/asset rendering.
