Gianfranco Marchetti

3D Consultant & Artist | Calcinate (BG), Italy

I’m a 3D artist specialized in modeling, texturing, rendering, and visual effects (VFX) using Blender as my primary tool.

I create realistic product visualizations, architectural renders, and detailed environments using efficient PBR workflows and advanced lighting setups.

My skills extend to creature and character modeling, rigging, and animation, with a focus on delivering expressive, believable, and technically optimized assets for both cinematic and real-time production.

My goal is to craft visually strong and technically clean 3D assets ready for games, animation, or marketing visualization — assets that combine aesthetic impact with production efficiency.

I’ve collaborated with an international video game development team, creating immersive 3D environments that refined my sense of composition, lighting, texturing, and visual storytelling.

As an active member of the Blender Italia Association and administrator of the Blender Italia User Group – Bergamo, I’m committed to promoting professional culture and innovation in 3D visualization.

I’m available for freelance collaborations and remote work — open to projects, partnerships, or commissions where design, storytelling, and technology meet.
