3D Consultant & Artist | Calcinate (BG), Italy

I’m a 3D artist specialized in modeling, texturing, rendering, VFX, creature & character modeling, and animations using Blender.

I create realistic product visualizations, architectural renders, and detailed environments using PBR workflows and advanced lighting setups.

My goal is to deliver visually strong and technically clean 3D assets ready for games, animation, or marketing visualization.

I’ve collaborated with an international video game team, creating immersive 3D environments and refining my skills in composition, lighting, texturing, and storytelling.

Active member of Blender Italia Association and administrator of Blender Italia User Group – Bergamo, promoting professional 3D art culture.
